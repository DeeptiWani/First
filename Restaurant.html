Our Story

Every Flavor Tells a Story

Just as every Indian has background which consist of mixtures and concoctions of races,Indian food has similar origins.
Pointing a finger at one particular type of cuisine is as difficult as pointing a finger and trying to identify one singular race as Indian.
The Flavors of India are especially dear to the world because of rich history,the varied mixture and the warm hospitality on the plate it is served in.India is thus 'Food for thought',for body,mind and soul.
